How to Flash

  1. Connect your Seeed XIAO ESP32-S3 to your computer via USB-C.
  2. Select Full (WiFi AP + WebUI) or Headless (mesh only) above.
  3. Click Connect & Flash and select the serial port for your ESP32 in the browser dialog.
  4. Wait for the flash to complete. The device will reboot automatically.
  5. Full firmware: Connect to the Antihunter WiFi AP (password: antihunt3r123) and open http://192.168.4.1.

Manual Flash (CLI)

If Web Serial is unavailable, use the shell script: 

curl -fLO https://github.com/lukeswitz/AntiHunter/raw/main/Dist/flashAntihunter.sh
chmod +x flashAntihunter.sh
./flashAntihunter.sh
